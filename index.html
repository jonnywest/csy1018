Web development

Wednesday, 02 January 2019

Purpose of this site

The purpose of this site is to demonstrate my understanding of the code on which websites are built and to show that I can design and implement code to create user-friendly web pages that conform to modern day web development conventions.

Web development is very new to me and has been quite daunting to jump into, but I have a keen interest in growing my skills and hope to be able to expand my knowledge in all facets of the industry.

"Web development has a very steep learning curve"

In this module I have learnt the basics of HTML and CSS which has at times been overwhelming, web development has a very steep learning curve, but I have also learnt to appreciate how the internet works as well as the relevance and underlying infrastructure of the World Wide Web.

A bit about me

I originally studied creative media production at college after I had finished school. Here I learnt about wide variety of subjects from radio broadcasting, print layout and sound design to film development, motion graphics and animation.

I then moved on to study games design where I was introduced to the basics of programming, world building and concept art. After completing my course, I spent a few years outside of education working full time before finally choosing to enrol on this computing degree to continue broadening my knowledge of all this technology.

Other site content

Second you will find a glossary showing definitions and explanations of web related terms. The third page will be a comparison of two web related jobs and my personal opinions on them, and then finally the tutorial page will display some web development techniques.
